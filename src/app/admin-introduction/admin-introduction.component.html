Welcome to the Admin Dashboard

Welcome to your centralized hub for managing and optimizing the educational experience. As an admin, you have access to powerful tools to oversee and enhance the learning environment.

Here, you can:

  • Manage Users: Add, update, and manage user profiles, including students and instructors. Ensure smooth operation by maintaining accurate and up-to-date user information.
  • Course Management: Create, edit, and organize courses. Monitor course progress and ensure that all educational materials are up-to-date and relevant.
  • Track Progress: Keep an eye on student progress with detailed analytics. View completion rates, module progress, and identify areas where additional support may be needed.
  • Review Feedback: Collect and analyze feedback from users to improve the overall educational experience. Use insights to make data-driven decisions and enhance course offerings.

Use the navigation menu on the left to access each of these features. Explore the tools and settings available to make the most out of your administrative role.
