  • Features

    The LearnMore platform empowers administrators to efficiently manage users, create courses, and monitor both progress and feedback. Student enrollment is automated, ensuring that employees are enrolled in courses tailored to their job roles and designations, streamlining the learning process.

  • About

    LearnMore is a comprehensive Learning Management System (LMS) designed to streamline and enhance the educational experience within organizations. Our platform is built to facilitate seamless course creation, user management, and progress tracking, empowering both administrators and learners alike. At LearnMore, we are committed to fostering continuous learning and development, ensuring that employees receive the training they need to excel in their roles. Our dedicated team of developers and educators work together to create an intuitive, user-friendly platform that adapts to the evolving needs of modern workplaces.
